Who Am I?

I'm Akhil Abraham George

In Short

Hi there! My name is Akhil Abraham George and I'm an SAP Consultant at Infosys. In my free time, I enjoy writing about technology on my blog. I have a passion for coding and all things tech-related, which led me to study computer science and engineering. Originally from Kerala, India, I currently reside at Kottayam.

Throughout my career, I've gained extensive experience in SAP Sales and Distribution, ABAP and Vistex. I'm constantly seeking to expand my knowledge and stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends, which is why I started my blog. I believe that technology has the power to transform businesses and improve people's lives, and I'm excited to be a part of that journey.

When I'm not working or writing, you can usually find me tinkering with new software or exploring the latest tech gadgets. I also enjoy spending time with family and friends, trying new foods, and traveling to new places. Thanks for stopping by my blog and feel free to reach out if you have any questions or just want to say hi!
